Most of us decide whether to watch a movie based on its box office performance, and sometimes we just want to see our favorite movie, actor, or director doing well.  
When you search for box office, you will find article after article and soon realize you have to check many articles to get any sense of how a movie is doing. Even then, reports from articles can be inaccurate or outdated.

And after going through many articles, your questions are still unanswered: How big was the opening? How is the movie doing internationally? Is the box office dropping? How were the weekend earnings?

<br />
If you want to avoid that and just want straight numbers, then [
<u>Box Office Mojo</u>](https://www.boxofficemojo.com/) is the place to go.

When you visit Box Office Mojo, on the homepage, you will see the most popular movies currently in theaters.  
Not only can you see all movies in theaters, but when you select a movie, it shows daily earnings from the release date with the percentage of drop or rise.
You have the option to select any country and see how the movie did there.

<br />

## Cool lists on Box Office Mojo:

[Worldwide - This year's and every past year's top 200 box office earners](https://www.boxofficemojo.com/year/world/)

[All-time - Top 1000 earners of all time (can sort by age rating)](https://www.boxofficemojo.com/charts/overall/)

[Franchise - Box office of every franchise (can sort movies within franchise)](https://www.boxofficemojo.com/franchise/)

[Brand - Movie studio box office](https://www.boxofficemojo.com/brand)

[Calendar - Movie release calendar](https://www.boxofficemojo.com/calendar/)

<br />

It's not just a place to see box office numbers, but also to discover all the movies that have done well in history. You will definitely find movies that you didn't know in that list.
